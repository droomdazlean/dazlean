Scope of the Academix School ERP

The School ERP system is designed to streamline administrative, academic, and operational tasks. It provides a centralized platform for managing students, teachers, staff, finances, and resources.

Module Features Notes
Student Management Admissions, Attendance, Academic Records, Disciplinary Issues Tracking Automates student-related processes like admissions and attendance tracking while ensuring secure storage of academic records.
Teacher Management Class Assignments, Notifications, Performance Appraisal Simplifies class scheduling for teachers, facilitates communication with students and parents, and tracks teacher performance metrics.
Fee & Finance Management Fee Collection, Payroll Processing, Financial Reporting School fee collections with online payment options, automates payroll processing, and provides financial insights for better decision-making.
Examination Management Exam Scheduling, Results Generation, Performance Analysis Reduces the complexity of exam planning by automating result generation and providing detailed performance analytics for students.
Library Management Book Inventory, Borrowed Books Tracking Tracks library resources efficiently, ensures smooth borrowing/returning processes, and provides search functionalities for books and journals.
Human Resource Management (HRM) Recruitment, Leave Management, Payroll Manages staff recruitment processes, tracks attendance and leave requests, and ensures seamless payroll processing.
Timetable Management Schedule Creation & Updates Automates timetable creation for classes and teachers while ensuring no conflicts in scheduling or resource allocation.
Transport Management Route Planning, Fleet Management Optimizes transport routes for buses and tracks vehicle maintenance records to ensure safety and efficiency.
Communication Portal Notifications, Announcements Facilitates communication between students, teachers, parents, and administrators through instant notifications and announcements.
Event Management Event Scheduling & Participant Tracking Organizes school events like sports days or cultural programs while tracking participant details efficiently.
Homework & Assignment Management Assignment Uploads by Teachers Allows teachers to upload assignments online while enabling students to submit their work digitally within deadlines.

Pricing Structure

To best suit your institution's financial and operational preferences, our School ERP system is available under two distinct pricing models: Subscription-Based Pricing and Perpetual Licensing.

Model Features Benefits Pricing
Subscription-Based Pricing
  • Payment is made monthly
  • Includes software updates, maintenance, and customer support
  • Scalable pricing based on the number of students and modules used
  • Low upfront costs
  • Flexibility to scale up or down
  • Always access the latest version
  • $2 USD per student monthly subscription
  • $200 USD per module per month
Perpetual Licensing
  • A one-time purchase gives lifetime ownership
  • Requires an annual maintenance contract (AMC) for updates and support
  • Often hosted on-premise
  • Long-term cost savings
  • Greater control over customization and hosting
  • No recurring subscription fees after the initial purchase
  • $3,500 USD upfront for full ERP system ownership
  • $600 annually for AMC covering support and updates

Additional Costs to Consider

  • Implementation Fees: One-time setup cost; $500.
  • Hosting Charges: Included in the charges above.
  • SMS/Email Communication: Recurring monthly expense based on usage volume ($0.01–$0.05 per message).
